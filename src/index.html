What is Underwater Hockey?

Underwater hockey (also known as Octopush) is a fast-paced team sport using a snorkel mask, fins and a short stick to push a hockey puck along the bottom of a pool.

It's fast, physical (but technically non contact!) and incredible for overall fitness.

The game is played on the bottom of the pool.

How do I join in?

Sessions take place at Plymouth Life Centre. All are welcome but you must be able to swim at least 50m.

Other sessions are available such as Plymouth Pirates and Kingsbridge Krays if you catch the bug!
